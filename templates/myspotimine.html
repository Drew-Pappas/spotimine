Welcome to Spotimine!

Spotimine imports your music library directly from Spotify to provide give you information about your library and to help generate playlists tailored to your preferences.

To view histograms of your library's traits like danceability, popularity of songs, or tempo, visit the library analytics section.

Go to library analyzer➜

To create custom playlists comprised of songs from your library based on library traits, visit the playlist generator section. You can even import generated playlists directly to spotify! Note, the playlist generator does not support filtering by artist or genre. Also, the accuracy of the playlist generator is partially dependent on the size of your library, so the larger the library the better!
